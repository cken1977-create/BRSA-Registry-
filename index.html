The Sovereign Registry of Behavioral and Organizational Readiness

The BRSA establishes, maintains, and verifies the readiness standing of individuals and institutions against a national behavioral standard. Readiness is not assumed. It is assessed, recorded, and publicly accountable.

What the Registry Is

A permanent public record of verified readiness standing

The BRSA Registry is the authoritative national record of readiness verification. Each entry reflects a formal assessment against the FRARI standard — the Federal Readiness and Readiness Index — conducted by a credentialed BRSA evaluator.

Registry entries are not self-reported. They are not assumed. They are assessed, scored, and assigned a canonical state. That state is publicly verifiable at any time.
